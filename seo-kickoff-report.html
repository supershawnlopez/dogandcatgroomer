AEO / GEO Layer (AI Search Readiness)

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)

  • Add FAQ blocks on services and contact pages (pricing ranges, appointment process, cat services).
  • Use concise Q/A headings that match spoken search questions.
  • Keep NAP, hours, and service area consistent across site and GBP.

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)

  • Publish structured service pages with explicit location entities.
  • Add LocalBusiness + Service schema for machine-readable entity clarity.
  • Build citations + mention consistency so AI systems see corroborated business facts.
